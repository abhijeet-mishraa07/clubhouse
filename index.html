About Us

Clubhouse is a platform designed to unify all college clubs into a single, user-friendly website. Our goal is to simplify the way students discover, join, and participate in clubs by offering a centralized space for communication, event updates, and collaboration. Whether it's academic, societies, sports teams, or cultural groups, Clubhouse helps bring together vibrant communities to foster engagement and growth.
